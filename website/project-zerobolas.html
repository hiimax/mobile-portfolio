The Challenge

Traditional second-hand marketplaces often lack engagement and trust, relying solely on static images. The challenge was to create a vibrant, video-first platform that makes buying and selling items locally feel as dynamic and trustworthy as a live interaction.

System Architecture

Built on a robust real-time infrastructure to support live video feeds, instant messaging, and secure transactions.

My Role & Solution

I led the mobile development using Flutter to deliver a seamless cross-platform experience. Key contributions included:

  • Video Shopping Experience: Integrated high-quality video playback and recording features to let sellers showcase products dynamically.
  • Real-time Interactions: Implemented instant messaging and notifications using Firebase to connect buyers and sellers immediately.
  • Performance Optimization: Optimized media loading and app startup times to ensure a smooth user experience even on lower-end devices.
