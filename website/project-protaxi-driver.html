ProTaxi Driver

ProTaxi is a ride-hailing app that prioritizes sustainability and affordability.

Vision & Challenge

To become the leading eco-conscious ride-hailing app, offering a sustainable and affordable transportation option for riders while promoting clean energy adoption.

The challenge was to build a platform that significantly reduces environmental impact by offering a primarily electric vehicle fleet while providing affordable ride-hailing services through cost-effective EV operations.

Objectives

  • Reduce environmental impact by offering a primarily electric vehicle fleet.
  • Provide affordable ride-hailing services through cost-effective Electric vehicle operations.
  • Offer a convenient and user-friendly mobile app for riders and drivers.
  • Streamline fleet management and operations through a robust admin backend.

Target Users

  • Riders: Individuals seeking eco-friendly and affordable on-demand transportation.
  • Drivers: Individuals seeking a flexible income opportunity focusing on sustainability.

System Architecture

Designed to reduce environmental impact by offering a primarily electric vehicle fleet and streamlining fleet management.
