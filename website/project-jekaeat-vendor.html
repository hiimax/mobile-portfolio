Jekaeat Vendor

Jekaeat Vendor is the management platform for restaurants and food vendors on the Jekaeat network.

Role Mobile Engineer
Timeline 2023 - Present

The Challenge

Jekaeat was established to address the challenges facing the food tech sector in Minna city, particularly the lack of a reliable platform for restaurants and food vendors to sell their food and connect with more food buyers and diners.

System Architecture

Designed a robust architecture to handle real-time orders, location tracking, and secure payments.

My Role & Solution

I developed the mobile application using Flutter, focusing on performance and user experience. My contributions included:

  • User Interface: Built a clean, intuitive UI for browsing menus and placing orders.
  • Location Services: Integrated Google Maps for accurate delivery address selection.
  • State Management: Implemented efficient state management to handle complex order flows.
