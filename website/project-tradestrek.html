The Challenge

Learning to trade stocks can be intimidating and risky, especially for beginners in Nigeria who may lack access to global markets or the capital to risk. The challenge was to create a safe, realistic environment where users could practice trading strategies, understand market dynamics, and build confidence without losing real money.

System Architecture

Designed a high-throughput simulator capable of processing real-time stock data and managing virtual portfolios for thousands of concurrent users.

My Role & Solution

I spearheaded the mobile application development, focusing on delivering a responsive and data-rich user interface. Key implementations included:

  • Realistic Trading Engine: Built features to simulate buy/sell orders with real-time price feeds, giving users a true-to-life trading experience.
  • Advanced Charting: Integrated interactive charts using Flutter libraries to allow users to perform technical analysis on stock trends.
  • Gamification: Implemented leaderboards and achievement systems to encourage learning and competition among users.
