Psych Insights

Personalised Mental Health Support. As we delved into today's mental health apps, we noticed a crucial element was missing: PERSONALISATION. Psych Insights is a personalised mental health self-help app designed to cater to your unique needs and goals.

The Challenge

For some, personal growth means overcoming anxiety or depression. For others, it's alleviating daily stress, improving sleep, or enhancing productivity. The challenge was to create a mental health app that moves beyond generic advice and offers true personalisation, adapting to each user's unique psychological profile and evolving needs alongside a qualified psychologist's evidence-based approach.

Built a scalable, AI-driven platform tailored to deliver personalized content and recommendations securely.

My Role & Solution

I led the mobile engineering efforts to translate the psychologist's vision into a seamless, user-friendly application. Key contributions included:

  • Personalisation Engine: Implemented the logic to tailor content based on evidence-based assessment tools and user inputs.
  • Content Delivery: Developed a robust system to serve diverse media types (articles, audio, video) efficiently.
  • User Experience: Crafted an intuitive and calming interface to ensure users can navigate their mental health journey with ease.
