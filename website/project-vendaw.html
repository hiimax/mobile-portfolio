My Role & Solution

As the Lead Engineer, I architected and built the mobile solution from ground up. I leveraged Flutter to create a high-performance, single-codebase app that feels native on both iOS and Android. Key implementations included:

  • Offline-First Design: Implemented local caching to allow merchants to record sales without internet, syncing automatically when online.
  • Real-time Inventory: Used Firestore listeners to update stock levels instantly across all devices.
  • Integrated Payments: Built a custom payment wrapper to handle diverse payment methods (Card, USSD, Transfer) seamlessly within the app.
