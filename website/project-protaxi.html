The Challenge

ProTaxi is a ride-hailing app that prioritizes sustainability and affordability. It offers electric vehicle transportation through a user-friendly mobile app for riders and drivers, with an efficient admin backend for management. ProTaxi aims to provide a seamless and eco-friendly transportation experience at competitive rates, leveraging the cost benefits of electric vehicles and strategic charging partnerships.

System Architecture

Designed to reduce environmental impact by offering a primarily electric vehicle fleet and streamlining fleet management.

My Role & Solution

To become the leading eco-conscious ride-hailing app, offering a sustainable and affordable transportation option for riders while promoting clean energy adoption.

We target riders seeking eco-friendly transportation and drivers seeking flexible income.

  • Environment: Reduce impact with primarily EV fleet.
  • Affordability: Cost-effective EV operations.
  • Convenience: User-friendly app for riders and drivers.
